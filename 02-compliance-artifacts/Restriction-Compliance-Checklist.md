# REACH Restriction Compliance Checklist

## 1. Purpose of This Checklist

This checklist is used to:
- Verify compliance with REACH restrictions (Annex XVII)
- Identify restricted substances in products and processes
- Prevent placing non-compliant materials on the EU market
- Support audits and regulatory inspections
