# Substance Inventory

## 1. Purpose of This Inventory

This **Substance Inventory** is maintained to:
- Identify and track all chemical substances used, manufactured, or imported
- Determine regulatory obligations under REACH and CLP
- Support SDS management, SVHC monitoring, and compliance decision-making
- Provide a single source of truth for audits and inspections

This inventory is a **living document** and must be kept up to date.

---

## 2. Scope

This inventory covers:
- Substances on their own
- Substances in mixtures
- Substances present in articles (where applicable)
- All suppliers and internal uses

---

## 4. Registration & Compliance Logic

For each substance, assess:
- Whether the 1-tonne threshold is exceeded
- Whether registration is required or an exemption applies
- Whether an Only Representative is involved (if non-EU supplier)
- Whether downstream user obligations apply

---

## 5. Link to Other Compliance Records

This inventory should be cross-referenced with:
- Safety Data Sheets (SDS / eSDS)
- REACH Registration Dossiers
- SVHC Monitoring Register
- SCIP notifications
- Customer declarations
- Internal risk assessments

---

## 6. Review & Maintenance

Recommended review frequency:
- Quarterly internal review
- Mandatory review after:
  - Supplier change
  - Composition change
  - Candidate List update
  - SDS revision
  - Volume increase
